Django Girls

If you are female and you want to learn how to build websites, we have good news for you! We are holding a one-day workshop for beginners!

It will take place during the PyCon Uganda conference, at Kampala, Uganda. We would like to give you an opportunity to learn how to program and become one of us - female programmers!

The workshop is free of charge to all attendees of PyCon Uganda 2025 and if you are interested in being part of this training, you can purchase a PyCon Uganda Ticket and get a Pass to the Django Girls Workshop!!

Welcome to the Django Girls Workshop at PyCon Uganda 2025!

If you are a woman and you have a laptop - through Pycon Uganda 2025, you will have the chance to meet with other women in the tech world. You don't need to know any technical stuff - The workshop is for people who are new to programming.

At the workshop, you will have access to:


Attend a One-Day Django Workshop

During the workshop, you will create your first website.

Meet Other Ladies in Tech

Get a chance to connect with other women in the tech world.

Interact with Organizers and Sponsors

Engage with the event organizers and some of the sponsors.

Django Girls Workshop FAQ

Do I need programming experience to join?

No! This workshop is for beginners. You don't need any prior knowledge. If you have some technical knowledge (like knowing what HTML or CSS are), you can still apply!

Should I bring my Own Laptop

Yes, please bring your own laptop. We don't provide hardware, and it's important for you to have your computer to take home everything you create during the workshop.

Do I need to Install anything on my Laptop before hand?

It would be helpful to have Python installed, but don't worry if you haven't done it yet. We'll have an installation session before the workshop to help you get set up.

Do I need to be a PyLady already to attend?

No. Simply sign up for the workshop session, and you're welcome to join other activities if you're interested.
