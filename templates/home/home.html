About The Conference

PyCon Uganda is one of the Python Conferences you can attend in World Wide. We shall talk about Python applications in data, web and other domains. This is a platform where Python users, developers, library developers and companies that use Python come together.

WHERE

Kampala, Uganda

(The Venue will be announced soon.)

Join us in the beautiful city of Kampala, Uganda, from the 6 - 10th August, 2025, where we congregate with hundreds of developers, researchers, students and other professionals across Africa and beyond to give talks, host workshops, tutorials, sprints, and together share our passion for community!
