PyLadies at PyCon Africa 2026

PyLadies is an international mentorship group focused on helping more women become active participants and leaders in the Python open-source community.

PyLadies also aims to provide a friendly support network for women and a bridge to the larger Python ecosystem. Anyone with an interest in Python is encouraged to participate! Meetups happen every third Saturday of the month.
