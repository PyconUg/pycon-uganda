Sponsorship Packages

Sponsorship of PyCon Africa will give your organisation global visibility and recognition. It is an excellent way to support the Python community, promote your products to our hundreds of delegates, and meet Python developers who are looking for work.

In return for your support, we will ensure that your company is given exposure before, during, and after the event, and we will work with you so that you get exactly what you're looking for out of sponsoring the conference.

Please contact sponsorship@pycon.africa if you are interested in sponsoring PyCon Africa 2026, or if you have any questions about sponsorship.

Please note that these packages are flexible - if you are interested in sponsoring, but none of our packages quite fit your needs or budget, our sponsorship team will be happy to work with you to customise your package benefits.

Regional Packages for African Organisations

You'll notice that most packages have 2 versions. The standard packages and Regional packages are both priced in US Dollars. The regional packages are there to make participation possible for African companies.
