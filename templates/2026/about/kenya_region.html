About the Kenya Community

Kenya has one of the most active Python ecosystems in Africa, with strong communities in software development, data science, AI, fintech, health tech, and open-source innovation. The Kenyan Python community plays a key role in shaping regional collaboration for PyCon Africa 2026.

Kenya's Role in PyCon Africa 2026

The Kenya regional team will:

  • Mobilize the local Python community for PyCon Africa 2026
  • Support speaker engagement, CFP submissions, and community outreach
  • Facilitate cross-border collaboration with Uganda and other regional teams

Kenya Local Team (Contacts)

For more information, partnerships, or participation inquiries, reach out to:

  • Python Kenya Community Leads
  • GDG & University Python Community Representatives
  • Regional Volunteer Coordinators

Regional Collaboration & Inclusion

PyCon Africa 2026 is built on Pan-African collaboration. These regional teams work closely with the main organizing committee to ensure:

  • Strong local representation
  • Inclusive participation across borders
  • Sustainable growth of Python communities in Africa
