PyCon Africa 2026 – Tanzania Region

About the Tanzania Community

Tanzania's Python community continues to grow across academia, startups, data analytics, civic technology, and digital transformation initiatives. PyCon Africa 2026 provides an opportunity to further elevate Tanzanian Python talent on a continental stage.

Tanzania's Role in PyCon Africa 2026

The Tanzania regional team will:

  • Engage developers, students, and tech hubs across Tanzania
  • Promote PyCon Africa participation and regional representation
  • Support workshops, community talks, and volunteer engagement

Regional Collaboration & Inclusion

PyCon Africa 2026 is built on Pan-African collaboration. These regional teams work closely with the main organizing committee to ensure:

  • Strong local representation
  • Inclusive participation across borders
  • Sustainable growth of Python communities in Africa
