Arriving by Bus to Kampala

Overview

Many international and regional attendees traveling to PyCon Africa 2026 may arrive in Kampala by long-distance bus. Major bus companies operate routes from Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, South Sudan, and the Democratic Republic of Congo, arriving at terminals located within Kampala's central business district.

Common arrival points include:

  • Namayiba Bus Terminal
  • Kisenyi Bus Park
  • Link Bus Terminal
  • Modern Coast / Mash Poa offices

What to Expect on Arrival

Bus terminals in Kampala are usually busy, especially during peak hours. You will find:

  • Ticket offices and company counters
  • Money exchange services nearby
  • Mobile money agents
  • Restaurants and small shops
  • Boda boda and taxi stages

Keep your luggage close and follow signage or ask uniformed staff for directions.

Getting to the PyCon Africa 2026 Venue

Once you arrive, you can connect to the conference venue using:

Ride-hailing apps

  • Uber
  • SafeBoda
  • Safecar

These are the most convenient and recommended options.

Special hire taxis

Available at or near bus terminals — agree on the fare before starting the trip.

Hotel shuttles

Some partner hotels may provide transport to the venue.

Estimated Travel Time

Travel time depends on traffic, but typical ranges from Kampala city center to major venues are:

  • 15 – 45 minutes off-peak
  • 30 – 90 minutes during rush hour

Safety Tips

  • Avoid displaying valuables openly.
  • Use registered taxis or ride-hailing apps.
  • Travel during daytime where possible.
  • If unsure, contact the PyCon travel desk.

Busy transport hubs require normal city travel precautions.
