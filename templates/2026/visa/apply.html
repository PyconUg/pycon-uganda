Applying for a Visa

All international visitors travelling to Uganda to attend PyCon Africa 2026, scheduled for October 2026 in Kampala, must ensure they meet Uganda's entry and visa requirements before travel.

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. Some travellers are visa-exempt, while others must apply for a visa online in advance.

Do I Need a Visa to Enter Uganda?

Most international travellers require a visa to enter Uganda.

Uganda operates an electronic visa (e-Visa) system, and eligible travellers must apply online and receive approval before travelling. Check official visa information and requirements here:

Uganda Directorate of Citizenship and Immigration Control

How to Apply for a Uganda Visa

All visa applications must be submitted through the official Uganda e-Visa portal. Apply for a visa online (official portal).

Application Steps

  • Visit the official e-Visa portal linked above.
  • Create an account and complete the online application form.
  • Select the appropriate visa type (e.g. Visitor/Tourist Visa).
  • Upload the required documents.
  • Pay the visa fee online.
  • Wait for approval by email.
  • Print the visa approval letter and travel with it.

Required Documents

When applying for a Uganda visa, you will typically need:

  • A valid passport (at least 6 months validity from date of entry)
  • A recent passport-size photograph
  • A copy of the passport bio-data page
  • Proof of travel (flight itinerary)
  • Accommodation details in Uganda
  • Yellow Fever vaccination certificate (recommended and may be required)
  • Conference invitation or registration confirmation (if requested)

Visa Processing Time

  • Visa processing typically takes 2–5 working days
  • Processing times may vary depending on nationality and application volume
  • Apply early — we recommend applying at least 4–6 weeks before travel

Visa Fees

Visa fees depend on the type of visa selected (single entry, multiple entry, or East African Tourist Visa). All fees are paid online during the application process via the official portal:

https://visas.immigration.go.ug/

Visa on Arrival

Uganda does not generally issue visas on arrival.

All travellers are strongly advised to apply online and obtain a visa approval letter before travelling. Airlines may deny boarding without proof of visa approval.

Visa-Exempt Countries

Nationals of some countries may enter Uganda without a visa for short stays. Visa exemptions are subject to change. Please confirm your status using official government sources:

Countries exempt from Ugandan Visas

Important Notes

  • Only use official Ugandan government websites ending in .go.ug
  • Avoid third-party or unofficial visa websites
  • Always travel with a printed copy of your visa approval letter
  • Entry requirements may change — check official sources close to your travel date

Official Uganda Government Links

  • Uganda Immigration Department: https://www.immigration.go.ug/
  • Official Uganda e-Visa Application Portal: https://visas.immigration.go.ug/

NB: Visa requirements and processes are determined by the Government of Uganda and may change. Attendees are responsible for ensuring they meet all entry requirements.
